ABOUT KATIE

Katie was born with a unique form of dwarfism. Known to face her challenges head-on, with a smile and a sense of humor, she had a personal mantra "Parva Sed Potens," Latin for "small but powerful".

PURPLE SHOES

The Purple Shoes Challenge is a competitive athletic event for adults and children (ages 6+) with physical and/or sensory limitations.

THE FOUNDATION

The mission of the Katie Lynch Foundation is to fund programs that promote opportunities and respect for people with disabilities, enabling them to lead active, fulfilling lives.
